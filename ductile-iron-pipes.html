Ductile Iron Pipes

Al Ustaz proudly extends its commitment to excellence into the realm of Ductile Iron Pipes. In an exclusive partnership with Jai Balaji Industries Limited, one of the top 10 steel manufacturers in India, Al Ustaz has secured a remarkable opportunity in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

Partnering with Jai Balaji is a significant milestone for Al Ustaz in the KSA region. This collaboration grants us exclusive rights to market, promote, distribute, and sell Jai Balaji's superior quality Ductile Iron Pipes, available in sizes ranging from DN100mm to DN1200mm.

Jai Balaji Industries Limited's dedication to producing world-class steel products perfectly aligns with Al Ustaz's commitment to excellence. Together, we are poised to revolutionize the ductile iron pipe market in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, setting new benchmarks for quality, reliability, and innovation.

Furthermore, our robust Ductile Iron Pipes are NWC approved for both Water and Sewage applications for the DN100 - DN1200 mm range, allowing our products to be especially qualified for any and all water applications.
