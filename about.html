ABOUT AL USTAZ

Al Ustaz Projects Limited Company

Welcome to Al Ustaz, a distinguished leader in the industry with a rich legacy spanning almost three decades. We began our journey as a pioneering formwork and scaffolding company in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia (KSA). Today, we proudly stand as a multifaceted organization encompassing five key divisions, each dedicated to excellence in its domain.

Al Ustaz has been purposefully structured to excel in a variety of domains, undertaking projects specialising in Scaffolding & Formworks, Ductile Iron Pipes, Security Fencing Works (Supply & Installation) and various other General Trading and Contracting projects.

We form strategic partnerships with renowned manufacturers and suppliers, catering to industries ranging from industrial and construction to marine and the oil & gas sector, thus seamlessly serving multiple sectors simultaneously.

A History of Resilience:

The Al Ustaz management and our dedicated employees boast extensive experience in Sales & Marketing.
